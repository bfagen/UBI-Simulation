Universal Basic Income Simulation

This is an interactive simulation of a small voluntary Universal Basic Income (UBI) program. A simulated population contributes a certain percent of their income to a common pool (such as 10%), and then that total pool is redistributed equally to all participants.
I view UBI as the ultimate form of solidarity. I hope that this type of small-scale voluntary program is a step towards the goal of a society where wealth and power is distributed equally. This program would provide support or even just convenience to people right here and right now, without having to get people elected or pass laws. With UBI there are no strings attached and everyone follows the same rules. You still benefit even if you usually pay more than you get back. Not only does a UBI support the community, but it can also serve as a form of insurance, so if you face some difficult circumstances like losing your job, you start getting payments. In fact, it is kind of like a general (non-field specific) union. UBI can allow people more time to job search, more free time, child support, and much more. Feel free to mess with this simulation all you want and let me know if you have any questions, concerns or ideas.
