Universal Basic Income Simulation

This interactive simulation models a small voluntary redistribution pool. A simulated population contributes a chosen percent of their income to a common fund, and the total pool is then redistributed according to simple household rules. Singles receive the full adult payout weight, while adults living with roommates or a shared-income partner receive a reduced payout weight.
