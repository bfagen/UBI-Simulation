Universal Basic Income Simulation

This interactive simulation models a small voluntary redistribution pool. A simulated population contributes a chosen percent of their effective monthly income to a common fund. Effective monthly income is income after monthly debt payments. The total pool is then redistributed equally according to simple household rules: singles receive the full adult payout weight, adults living with roommates or a shared-income partner receive a reduced payout weight, and each child under 18 adds extra payout weight.
